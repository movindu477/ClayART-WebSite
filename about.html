About HNS Chamber

HNS Chamber wholeheartedly welcomes and offers you a variety of unique wall art creations and home accents. Every piece is unique and timeless to elevate the ambience of your precious space. Explore all three Studios to find the favorite piece that compliments your room’s aesthetic.


Air Dry Clay Studio

Clay on Canvas wall decor and other clay-based home accents are carefully handcrafted with natural air dry clay and painted with acrylic. I have spent lengthy hours to bring out the uniqueness in every piece given its delicate nature. Each petal is orchestrated in a manner to highlight the beauty of imperfections.

Photographic Studio

Every wall picture you find here has been captured by myself. I sincerely hope that it would add the feel of healing energy as well as character to your room.


Founder’s Message

Thank you for visiting HNS chamber.
The idea of establishing HNS Chamber happened during a critical phase of my life and it took longer than anticipated given challenging circumstances. I always believe a warm and an adorning living space makes us more connect with ourselves and feels to be around with loved ones, so that’s where my passion started for creating wall decor and home accents. My desire to work with Clay helped me to create unique wall arts and designs by amalgamating Clay on Canvas. While mastering my new found technique, I did let my artistry thoughts run freely to design variety of timeless pieces which adds elegance. Once clay art work is established, I decided to expand my scope to wall paintings as well as to make wall art using my own photography. HNS Chamber wishes to add more elements in future to make your space closer to your heart. I sincerely hope that you may find a piece that touches your heart! Thank you and may you have a wonderful day ahead!! Hansika Subasinghage Founder of HNS Chamber
